How It Works

Five automated steps. Zero manual monitoring.

1
📡

Monitor

Pulls continuously from EUR-Lex, ECHA, and national EPR registers. Live RSS feeds + pre-parsed snapshots as fallback.

2
🤖

Understand

IBM Bob (Watsonx Granite) reads each update, extracts what changed, which regulation, the deadline, and affected product categories.

3
🎯

Match

Maps each regulatory change to your specific partners and products using a scored multi-factor matching engine. High precision, low false positives.

4
📲

Alert

Sends an actionable notification via WhatsApp, SMS, or email: "New rule X applies to your product Y. Deadline Z. Here's what to do."

5
📊

Track

Dashboard shows your risk portfolio at a glance. Audit log of every alert. Acknowledgement tracking. Full compliance trail.

Regulations We Track

The EU rules that catch electronics SMEs off-guard

⚠️ CRITICAL

EU Battery Passport

Regulation (EU) 2023/1542
📅 Deadline: 18 Feb 2027

Mandatory digital passport for all industrial, EV, and LMT batteries. QR code + lifecycle data required.

Affects: Lithium batteries · EV components · Energy storage
🔶 HIGH

PPWR

Regulation (EU) 2025/40
📅 Deadline: 12 Aug 2026

Mandatory recyclability criteria and recycled content minimums for all EU packaging.

Affects: Plastic packaging · Consumer goods · Textiles
⚠️ CRITICAL

RED Cybersecurity

Delegated Reg. (EU) 2022/30
📅 Deadline: 1 Aug 2025

All internet-connected radio devices must meet cybersecurity, privacy, and anti-fraud requirements under RED.

Affects: IoT devices · Wireless · Consumer electronics
🔶 HIGH

CBAM

Regulation (EU) 2023/956
📅 Full pricing: 1 Jan 2026

Carbon border adjustment for steel, aluminium, cement and other carbon-intensive imports into the EU.

Affects: Steel · Aluminium · Carbon-intensive goods
🔶 HIGH

REACH SVHC Update

REACH (EC) 1907/2006 — Jan 2025
📅 Notify by: 14 Jul 2025

3 new SVHCs added to the Candidate List. SCIP notification required if >0.1% w/w in articles.

Affects: Chemical substances · Coatings · PCBs
